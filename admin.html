Misr El Kheir

  • MEK is a non-profit development institution established in 2007 with the objective of developing the Egyptian individual in a comprehensive manner. Five key areas have been selected for human development namely: Health, Education, Scientific Research, Social Solidarity and Aspects of Life.

View Details

Children's Cancer Hospital 57357

  • The Children's Cancer Hospital Egypt 57357 widely known as 57357 is a unique healthcare institution. The people of Egypt and friends from all over the world have contributed generously to the establishment of the hospital which has been completely built by donations.

View Details

Egyptian Red Crescent

  • Egyptian Red Crescent works to achieve the ultimate goal of alleviating humanitarian sufferings especially for those in crisis. It is guided by the seven principles of the movement; humanity, impartiality, Neutrality, Independence, Voluntary Service, Unity and Universality.

View Details

Egyptian Food Bank

  • The EFB was founded in 2006 by a group of businessmen with the aim of serving the society and helping those in need by specializing in Hunger under the name of Egyptian Food Bank that would be converted to a national project that serves all governorates in Egypt.

View Details

Resala

  • Resala was established in 1999. It is powered by over 1,500,000 volunteers spanning 67 branches all over Egypt. It provides activities including: caring for orphans, helping the blind, the deaf, and children with special needs, blood donations; poverty alleviation; and literacy training.

View Details

Al-Orman

  • Al Orman is an Egyptian non-profit organization. It was founded in 1993. The organization is serving those in need without any religious or political bias. It depends primarily on financial funds from Egyptians whether living in Egypt or abroad.

View Details

Marwa Fayed's Toy Run

  • In the loving memory of Marwa Fayed, Marwa Fayed's Toy Run was initiated. MFTR is a small organization with one single aim, spreading joy to underprivileged children in Egypt and abroad by distributing toys to them.

View Details

Sawiris Social Development Foundation

  • SFSD was established with an endowment from the Sawiris Family. Since its establishment, SFSD focused on addressing the most pressing issues facing the most marginalized Egyptians such as poverty, unemployment, provision of health services, and little access to quality education.

View Details

Magdi Yacoub Foundation

  • MYF is an Egyptian registered charity NGO that was founded in 2008 by Sir Magdi Yacoub, the late Dr. Ahmed Zewail and Ambassador Mohamed Shaker. MYF is running one of the exceptional projects of enormous significance to the health and wellbeing of the Egyptian people that is entirely based on donations.
